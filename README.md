# 📌 Project Overview

Customer Funnel Analysis is one of the most important analytical techniques used in E-Commerce to understand how customers move through each stage of the buying journey.

This interactive Tableau dashboard analyzes the complete customer journey—from browsing products to completing a purchase—to uncover where customers abandon the process, identify revenue leakage, and provide actionable business insights for improving conversion rates.

The dashboard is designed for business users, marketing teams, and decision-makers to monitor customer behavior through an intuitive and interactive visualization.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Identify customer drop-off points across the purchase funnel
- Measure conversion performance
- Analyze bounce rates across acquisition channels
- Compare regional and product category performance
- Identify the best-performing acquisition channels
- Discover revenue leakage opportunities
- Enable data-driven marketing and business decisions

# 📈 Key Insights

### Customer Conversion

- Total Customers: **10,000**
- Conversion Rate: **10.8%**
- Nearly **89%** of customers leave before completing a purchase.

---

### Funnel Performance

The largest customer drop-off occurs between:

```
Checkout
      ↓
Purchase
```

Drop-off Rate:

**69.4%**

This indicates significant revenue leakage during the final stage of the purchasing process.

---

### Bounce Rate

Overall Bounce Rate:

**89.2%**

Highest Bounce Channel:

✅ Email

This suggests that landing page optimization or campaign targeting may require improvement.

---

### Best Performing Product Category

🏆 Electronics

Conversion Rate:

**11.19%**

---

### Best Acquisition Channel

🏆 Email

Conversion Rate:

**11.21%**

---

### Best Performing Region

🏆 East

Conversion Rate:

**11.26%**
